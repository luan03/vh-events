Leap

What is VanHack Leap?

VanHack Leap is an in-person event held across cities in Canada and Europe where companies looking to hire senior tech talent can meet 30-50 top developers – who’ve flown in from all over the world – interview them, and hire them!

Getting a better job is not the only motivation for them to make the trip to a Leap Event. These candidates are looking to change their lives, and are extremely motivated to be successful in their new job and new country.

Recruiting Mission

VanHack Recruiting Missions is back for its first stop in 2020

2019 has been a great year for VanHack events, but 2020 will be HUGE! We are having more editions of Leap and more editions of the Recruiting Mission in different cities and countries. This post is to announce that the first stop of VanHack Recruiting Mission 2019 will be in São Paulo, Brazil – and you can’t miss it!

VanHack is bringing to São Paulo on March 23rd and 24th a Recruiting Mission for Tech Talents who want to fuel the growth of Canada’s and Europe’s tech market. Some of the best companies in North America and Europe will be attending the fair, in hopes of filling over 100 positions.

VanHackathon

The biggest hackathon in the world

The VanHackathon is for developers and designers who want to get hired abroad. We’ll also have 3-5 companies from Canada and Europe who are looking for great tech talent to add to their teams.

Also, we’ll be having a great company from Canada that is hiring 300 developers in 90 days. They need React, Java, DevOps and Machine Learning engineers.

Each company will have their own way to judge what is good code. They will share what they are looking for in the Opening Hangout.
